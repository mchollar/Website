Privacy

Privacy Policy

The short version: our apps don’t collect personal data, and we never sell or share your information with third parties. The details, app by app, are below.

ReadingTime — Family Tracker

ReadingTime stores only the data you enter: reader names, books, reading sessions, ratings, and notes. Starting with version 2.0, this data is synced to Apple’s iCloud via CloudKit, so it is available across all devices signed in to your iCloud account. The app’s Family Sharing feature lets you optionally share an individual reader’s shelf with another iCloud user, such as another family member.

Data we collect

Only the data you choose to enter — reader names, books, reading times, ratings, and notes. No usage analytics tied to you, no advertising identifiers, no location data.

Third parties
  • Apple CloudKit — used to sync your data across your devices. Subject to Apple’s Privacy Policy.
  • Google Books API — used when you search for books by title or author. Only your search query is sent; no personal data is transmitted.
  • TelemetryDeck — used to collect anonymized, non-personal usage analytics (such as which features are used and app version). No personally identifiable information is collected. Subject to TelemetryDeck’s Privacy Policy.

No other third-party services are used.

Data retention and deletion

Your data lives on your device and in your iCloud account. You can delete individual entries from within the app at any time. To remove your data from iCloud entirely, go to Settings → [your name] → iCloud on your device. Deleting the app removes local data; iCloud data must be removed separately through your iCloud settings.

Lectios

Apple’s iOS App Store requires that we provide the following information regarding Lectios:

What data it collects

Lectios only collects the data you enter and stores it on the device in a local cache, which is backed up to iCloud if you’re logged in. This lets all devices on your iCloud account stay in sync.

Third-party sharing

Lectios does not share information of any sort with any other party or service.

Data retention and deletion

There is no need for a separate retention or deletion policy — your data is yours. If you want to delete it from your device and iCloud account, that’s entirely up to you; it is not stored anywhere else.

NumberBuilder

Apple’s iOS App Store requires that we provide the following information regarding NumberBuilder:

What data it collects

NumberBuilder does not access any private user data, nor does it store any data.

Third-party sharing

NumberBuilder does not share information of any sort with any other party or service.

Data retention and deletion

NumberBuilder doesn’t store any data. Deletion is entirely up to the user.

Airmanship Help Button

Apple’s iOS App Store requires that we provide the following information regarding Airmanship Help Button:

What data it collects

Airmanship Help Button does not access any private user data. The only data stored is what the user enters, in the form of phone numbers and names.

Third-party sharing

Airmanship Help Button does not share information of any sort with any other party or service.

Data retention and deletion

Airmanship Help Button only stores data locally on the user’s device. Deletion is entirely up to the user.
