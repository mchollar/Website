What it is

Like Goodreads, but just for your family.

A private reading tracker for your whole family — no social network, no public reviews. Just your family's books and reading history in one place.

Every reader gets their own shelf with Currently Reading, Want to Read, and Already Read. Kids can rate books and add notes. Parents can log sessions, run a timer while reading, and generate PDF reports — perfect for school reading programs.

Features
Track books read, dates, minutes spent, and total pages for every reader.
Generate a report you can save as PDF or print right from your device.
Use a built-in timer or stopwatch as you read — or just log the time afterward.
Add books by scanning the barcode, manual entry, or searching the web (Google Books).
Individual readers can rate their books and add notes.
