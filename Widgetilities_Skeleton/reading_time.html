ReadingTime

Multi-User Book Tracking

Description

Reading Time allows you to track the reading of multiple people.
A must-have for parents who want to track their kids' reading history!
Great for reading programs or just general reading tracking for your own purpose.

Free to try!

Full Unlock In-App Purchase for access to full features for just $1.99

Features

  • Easily track number of books read, when they read them, how many minutes they spent reading, how many total pages.
  • Generate a handy report that you can save as PDF or print right from your device
  • Use a built-in timer or stopwatch as you read, or just log the amount of time after the fact
  • Import books either by scanning the barcode, manual entry or searching the internet (powered by Google Books)
  • Individual readers can rate their books and add notes, so you can track what they do and don't like

What's New

Several updates to the interface such as:

  • Add books to reader's list from within the book detail view in the library
  • Moved the 'Add Book' button to the top of the list in readers' shelves
  • Ability to collapse and expand reader's shelves (for those who have extensive lists!)
  • Other small appearance improvements
