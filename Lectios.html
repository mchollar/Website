Description

Lectios will help you stay on track with your self-guided lesson plan.
It does calculations to determine what's needed to stay on track and gives you a visual depiction of your progress in a graph.
No more doing math to try to figure out if you're ahead or behind schedule.
Lectios does it for you!

Features

  • For each subject you want to track:
    • Set up the calendar with start and end dates, and select which days you plan to work and which ones will be off. (Holidays, non-school days, etc.)
    • If you have multiple subjects with the same calendar, you can build it once and then copy the calendar to the others.
    • As you complete lessons during the semester, update your progress and see the results!

  • Share data with other users, no sign-in or account required! (As long as you're signed in to iCloud).
  • Parents, share with your kids and keep track of their progress!
  • Lectios also uses iCloud to sync across all of your own iOS and iPadOS devices on your account.
  • Generate a PDF report to capture a summary. Handy for documenting course completion.
  • Free to try - limited functionality until full unlock purchased.
  • Great for homeschool students, teachers, or anyone who has a self-guided lesson plan they want to follow and hit their desired goal on time!
