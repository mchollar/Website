Lectios

Track and forecast your self-guided lesson progress.

For homeschoolers & educators
Free to try · full unlock available
What it is

Stay on pace, without the math.

Lectios helps you stay on track with your self-guided lesson plan. It does the calculations to determine what's needed to stay on schedule and shows your progress in a clear graph.

No more doing math to figure out if you're ahead or behind — Lectios does it for you. Great for homeschool students, teachers, or anyone following a self-guided plan who wants to hit their goal on time.

Features
Set up a calendar per subject with start and end dates, plus working and off days for holidays and breaks.
Build a calendar once and copy it to other subjects that share the same schedule.
Update progress as you complete lessons and instantly see where you stand.
Share data with other users — no sign-in or account required, just iCloud. Parents can follow their kids' progress.
Syncs across all your iOS and iPadOS devices via iCloud.
Generate a PDF report to document course completion.
