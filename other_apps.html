Airmanship Help Button

My commander asked me to develop an app that would allow squadron members to contact him for help with one tap of a button. I also included useful telephone numbers in case of emergency such as chaplain, mental health, etc.

Soaring Status

This app facilitates communication between operations supervision and flight line operations at the USAFA Airfield. It syncs sortie information and updates crews on current flying status while also pushing the latest status to Twitter.
